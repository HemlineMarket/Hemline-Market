Hemline Market is where fabric finds new life.

A marketplace for fabric lovers.

As sewists, we already have the vision, the skill, and the tools to bring ideas to life. But the rate-limiting step is always the same:

Where is the fabric?

Hemline Market was created to solve that: a community marketplace where makers connect directly to buy and sell fabric. From forgotten treasures to fresh finds, it’s where the right fabric finally meets the right maker.

We believe in slow fashion, sustainable choices, and community care, helping each yard of fabric have the chance to become something new.

I’m a self-proclaimed fabric hoarder who loves imagining what each piece can become: the drape, the weight, the texture, and the story waiting to unfold. Hemline Market is born from that love of textiles and from the belief that every fabric deserves its chance to be transformed.
