About Hemline Market

Hemline Market was created for sewists who care about slow fashion, sustainability, and sharing the fabrics we love. Whether it is quilting cotton, silk charmeuse, or wool gabardine, passing materials along helps reduce waste and spark creativity.

Think of Hemline Market as similar to eBay or Poshmark, but for sewists, with prepaid shipping labels, secure Stripe payments, and built-in purchase protection that helps everyone feel safe listing and buying fabric.

ThreadTalk, our sewing forum, is a place to connect, share ideas, and grow together as makers.

This is the first version of Hemline Market, and we are just getting started. More tools and features are on the way, and we are genuinely excited to receive your feedback as we improve Hemline Market together.

The first 300 verified sellers will lock in reduced selling fees for life. Early supporters deserve perks.
