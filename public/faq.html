Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell on Hemline Market?

Create a listing, upload your fabric details, and once it sells you’ll be emailed a shipping label. Print, attach, and ship your item—it’s that simple.

Who ships the order?

The seller ships the order. A prepaid shipping label is emailed directly to the seller after a sale.

Who pays for shipping?

The buyer pays for shipping at checkout.

Can I offer local pickup?

No. Local pickup is not supported, since it falls outside of Hemline Market’s protections for both buyers and sellers.

Do you support international sales?

Currently, Hemline Market is available only within the United States.

Can I sell bundles?

Yes! Buyers can purchase multiple fabrics together as a bundle.

Are my purchases and sales protected?

Yes. Hemline Market holds payment until the order is confirmed delivered and protects both sides from fraud or misrepresentation.

Do you accept returns?

Returns are not accepted except in cases where an item arrives not as described or significantly damaged. Please review each listing carefully before purchase.
