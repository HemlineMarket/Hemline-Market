Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell on Hemline Market?

List your fabric from the Sell page. Add photos (at least one), a title, price per yard, and yards available, then complete the details. When it sells, you’ll be emailed a prepaid shipping label—print it, pack it, and drop it off.

Who ships the order?

Sellers ship. We email the seller a prepaid label as soon as the buyer checks out.

Who pays for shipping?

Buyers pay at checkout. A prepaid label is generated and sent to the seller by email.

Can I do local pickup?

No. For safety and platform protection, we don’t support local pickup.

Do you support international orders?

Not yet—U.S. only for now.

Can I sell bundles or remnants?

Yes. You can list single cuts, bundles, or remnants—just be clear about yardage and any defects.

How are purchases and sales protected?

Payments are held until tracking shows the package is on its way. We keep communication and transactions on-platform to help resolve issues quickly.

When do sellers get paid?

Payouts are initiated after the carrier scans the shipment. Transfer timing depends on your bank.

Do you accept returns?

We don’t support change-of-mind returns. If an item arrives not as described, contact Support within 3 days of delivery.

Looking for policies? See Terms and Privacy. Shipping specifics are in the item checkout flow.
