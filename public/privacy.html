Privacy Policy

Plain-language overview of how your information is handled on this site.

1) What We Collect

  • Basic site logs: standard analytics data (pages viewed, device/browser info, approximate region).
  • Order info (Phase 2): name, email, shipping address, and order details to fulfill purchases.
  • Messages: when you email us via hello@hemlinemarket.com.

2) How We Use It

  • To run the website and improve the browsing experience.
  • To process and ship orders (Phase 2).
  • To respond to your messages and support requests.

3) Sharing

  • We don’t sell your data.
  • We may share limited info with service providers to operate the site (hosting) and, in Phase 2, to process payments and deliveries (e.g., Stripe, shipping carriers).
  • We may share information if required by law.

4) Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to understand site traffic and performance. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

5) Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6) Your Choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing us.
  • You can opt out of non-essential analytics cookies in your browser.

7) Contact

Email hello@hemlinemarket.com for privacy questions.
