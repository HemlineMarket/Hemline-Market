Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This page explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

What we collect

When you browse Hemline Market, we may collect basic analytics (pages visited, device type, approximate location) to improve the site. If you create an account or make a purchase, we collect information you provide such as name, email, shipping address, and transaction details.

How we use information

  • To operate and improve Hemline Market.
  • To process orders and payments.
  • To communicate with you about your account, orders, or support requests.
  • To prevent fraud and enforce our Terms.

Sharing

We don’t sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who help us run Hemline Market (e.g., hosting, payments, email), and only for the services they provide. We require them to protect your data.

Cookies

We use cookies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and measure site performance.

Your choices

  • You can request a copy or deletion of your data by emailing hello@hemlinemarket.com.
  • You can unsubscribe from marketing emails via the link in those emails.

Data retention

We keep information only as long as necessary to provide the service and meet legal obligations.

Children

Hemline Market is not intended for children under 13. If we learn we’ve collected data from a child, we’ll delete it.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@hemlinemarket.com.
