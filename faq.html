Frequently Asked Questions

Short and clear. We’ll expand as we learn from buyers and sellers.

What is Hemline Market?

A curated marketplace for fabrics and sewing materials.

How do I buy?

Browse listings, add to cart, and check out. Sellers ship directly to you. Flat-rate shipping shows at checkout.

How do I sell?

Create a seller account, upload details and photos, set a price, and publish. Print a prepaid label after a sale.

Returns?

Most fabric sales are final. If an item is misrepresented, email us for a quick resolution.

Fees?

Transparent and shown before listing goes live and on order summaries.
