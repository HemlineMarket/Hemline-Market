Frequently Asked Questions

This page is a placeholder. Add real Q&A as buyers and sellers ask them.

Can I return fabric I don’t want?

No. All sales are final unless the item is not as described (e.g., wrong fiber, width, or undisclosed flaws).

How much is shipping?

Flat-rate shipping is applied at checkout. Rates are designed to be predictable and affordable.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, though buyers are responsible for customs duties and taxes in their country.

How do I list fabric for sale?

Create an account, click List Item, and fill in the seller form with fabric details.

What payment methods are accepted?

Major credit/debit cards are accepted via our payment processor. Sellers are paid out through linked accounts.

Is this site safe?

Yes. Hemline Market uses secure connections, encrypted payments, and moderation of reported content.
