Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for buyers and sellers. Placeholder content.

How do I buy fabric on Hemline Market?
Browse items, open the listing page, and follow the checkout prompts. In Phase 1, the cart/checkout is disabled — listings are placeholders.
What are the shipping timelines?
Sellers ship within 2–4 business days. All orders receive tracking — see Order Tracking.
What is the return policy?
All sales are final unless the item is not as described. See Shipping & Returns for details.
