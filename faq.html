Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Buying

How do I buy fabric?
Browse listings and use the checkout button to complete your purchase securely.

What payment methods are supported?
We support credit cards via Stripe. More options coming soon.

Selling

How do I list fabric?
Create a seller account and upload fabric details and photos from your dashboard.

Can I sell handmade items?
Yes — we welcome handmade sewing items alongside fabrics.

Shipping

Who ships the order?
Sellers are responsible for packaging and shipping directly to buyers.

Is there flat-rate shipping?
Yes — Hemline integrates with Shippo to offer flat rates.
