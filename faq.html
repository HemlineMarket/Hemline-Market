Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. If you don’t find what you need, contact us here.

Are sales final?
Yes. All sales are final unless a listing is not as described (wrong fiber/width/length, undisclosed flaws). See Shipping & Returns.
How do I list a fabric?
Go to List Item and fill in title, price per yard, yardage, fiber, width, and description. Photos are placeholders in Phase 1.
What shipping options are available?
Sellers choose a processing time and use trackable shipping when available. International buyers cover duties/taxes. Details in Shipping & Returns.
How do bundles work?
Bundles let you combine multiple listings from a seller to save on shipping. Create and manage bundles on the Bundles page.
What if the color looks different in person?
Minor monitor-to-fabric variance is expected and not refundable. Sellers should describe color precisely; buyers can ask for more photos via Contact.
Where do I manage my account info?
Update name, email, and defaults in Settings. Orders and favorites live under Orders and Favorites.
