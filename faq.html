Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell on Hemline Market?

Create a listing, upload your fabric details, and once it sells you’ll be emailed a shipping label. Print, attach, and ship your item.

Who ships the order?

The seller ships the order. A prepaid shipping label is emailed to the seller after a sale.

Who pays for shipping?

The buyer pays for shipping at checkout.

Can I offer local pickup?

No. Local pickup is not supported, since it’s outside Hemline Market’s protections.

Do you support international sales?

Not yet — the marketplace is currently US-only.

Can I sell bundles?

Yes. Buyers can purchase multiple fabrics together.

Are my purchases and sales protected?

Yes. Payment is held until delivery is confirmed and both sides are protected from fraud or misrepresentation.

Do you accept returns?

Returns aren’t accepted except when an item arrives not as described or significantly damaged.
