Returns & Refunds

How returns are handled on Hemline Market.

Overview

Returns are managed by individual sellers. Each listing may include its own return policy. If a listing does not specify a policy, the item is considered final sale.

Requesting a return

  1. Locate your order on the Orders page.
  2. Contact the seller with your order number and reason for return.
  3. Follow the seller’s instructions for packaging and shipping the item back.

Refunds

Refunds are issued after the seller confirms receipt and inspection of the returned item. Processing times may vary depending on your payment method.

Damaged or incorrect items

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact the seller within 48 hours with photos so they can assist you promptly.

Need help?

If you cannot reach the seller, email support@hemlinemarket.com and include your order number.
