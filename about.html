About Hemline Market

Hemline Market is a fabric-first marketplace created by sewists for sewists. Our mission is to make finding atelier-worthy textiles simple and joyful.

What we believe

  • Quality over quantity: Only curated listings that inspire.
  • Transparency builds trust: Clear fiber content, yardage, and images.
  • Community matters: Sellers showcase their finds; buyers feel confident.

Roadmap

Expect filters, favorites, email notifications, and seamless shipping. We’re just getting started—feedback is always welcome.
