About Hemline Market

Hemline Market is built for creators who treat fabric as the raw material of art. Our mission is to make it simple, safe, and inspiring to buy and sell fabrics online.

Our Philosophy

We believe every piece of fabric carries a story. Whether it’s Italian wool, Japanese denim, or a rare remnant — we want those stories to find new makers.

For Buyers

Search by content, weight, color, and more. Save favorites, follow sellers, and discover fabrics you won’t find at big-box stores.

For Sellers

Listing is simple: upload details, set your price, and ship directly. Our flat-rate shipping and “final sale” clarity keep transactions smooth.

Looking Ahead

This is just Phase 1. Future phases will bring advanced seller dashboards, bulk tools, bundles, and global reach.
