About Hemline Market

Hemline Market is a curated fabric marketplace designed for makers, designers, and ateliers. Our goal is to make sourcing couture-grade textiles simple, enjoyable, and transparent.

Our Vision

We believe in slow fashion and thoughtful craft. Every yard listed here carries a story — whether it’s a remnant from a designer studio, a bolt of deadstock, or a rare vintage find.

How It Works

  • Sellers list fabrics with clear details on fiber, weight, and care.
  • Buyers use powerful filters to find exactly what they need.
  • Flat-rate shipping keeps checkout simple and predictable.

Contact Us

If you have questions, ideas, or feedback, please get in touch. We’re building this marketplace with you in mind.
