Shipping & Returns

Policy placeholder — reflects “no returns unless wrong description/not as described.”

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed in 1–3 business days. Seller-specific processing times appear on listings.

Shipping Rates

  • Flat-rate options may be shown at checkout.
  • Combined shipping for multiple items from the same seller when possible.
  • Tracking provided for most destinations.

International Shipping

Buyers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees charged by their country.

Returns

No returns. Due to cutting/handling of textiles, all sales are final. The only exception is if an item is not as described (e.g., wrong fiber/width/length, undisclosed flaws).

Report Not-as-Described (7 days)

  1. Contact us within 7 days of delivery.
  2. Include your order number and clear photos (label, fabric, flaw/issue).
  3. We’ll review and, if confirmed, offer a refund (full or partial) or other remedy.

What Isn’t Covered

  • Buyer’s remorse or over-ordering
  • Minor color variation due to screen differences
  • Issues reported after 7 days from delivery

Exchanges

No exchanges.
