Shipping & Returns

Details about delivery and returns.

Shipping

Orders are shipped directly by individual sellers. Shipping rates are calculated using Hemline’s Shippo integration, offering reliable flat-rate options.

Delivery Time

Most orders are processed within 3–5 business days. Delivery times vary by seller location and buyer destination.

Returns

Returns are handled by individual sellers. Please review the seller’s policy before purchase. If no policy is listed, the order is considered final sale.
